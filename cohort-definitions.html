Marketing Mailers

Cohort Definitions

The shared dictionary every mailer, ad, and landing page targets. Each cohort below names a precise lifecycle stage, defines exactly who lands in it, and states the audience and the trigger that should convert them. Use these names verbatim in briefs so copy, imagery, and offers stay consistent across channels.

How cohorts are assigned: recency = days since last order · frequency = lifetime order count · monetary = AOV / lifetime value, computed from the order data in Data Analysis. A customer sits in exactly one cohort at a time and moves between them as behaviour changes.
